Delete Your MedExcel Account

You can permanently delete your MedExcel account directly from within the app.

Steps to delete your account:

  1. Open the MedExcel app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your action

Once deleted, your account and associated data will be permanently removed, including:

  • Your profile information
  • Generated flashcards and MCQs
  • Progress and activity data

If you are unable to access your account, you can request deletion by contacting us at:

medxcel@gmail.com

We process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.
